# AI vs Human Benchmark Protocol

## Objective

Measure how effectively AI systems solve or reason about historically difficult mathematical problems.

## Evaluation Levels

Level 1: Problem Understanding
Level 2: Known Technique Identification
Level 3: Proof Reconstruction
Level 4: Novel Reasoning
Level 5: Research Contribution

## Scoring

- Correctness: 40%
- Reasoning Quality: 30%
- Verification Success: 20%
- Efficiency: 10%

## Human Baselines

- Undergraduate Students
- Graduate Students
- PhD Researchers
- Domain Experts

## AI Baselines

- Frontier Models
- Open Source Models
- Specialized Mathematical Systems
